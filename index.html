FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know before getting started.
What is Stratford Academy?▾

Stratford Academy is an automated futures trading platform with 4 proven strategies across NASDAQ (NQ/MNQ) and S&P 500 (ES/MES) futures. Our algorithms trade directly on your broker account — fully hands-off, 24/5.

Do I need trading experience?▾

No. Our platform is built for complete beginners. We have a Beginner Learning Center with 5 free guides (futures pairs, prop firms, indicators, automated trading, and terminology), a full setup guide, and a free forever paper demo so you can learn and test without risking real money.

Which brokers are supported?▾

We support Tradovate (recommended), NinjaTrader, and Rithmic. We're also compatible with prop firms including Apex Trader Funding, TopStep, and My Funded Futures.

Is the paper demo really free?▾

Yes, 100% free forever. The paper demo uses simulated trades — no real money, no broker credentials needed. Your paper account keeps trading indefinitely — go live whenever you're ready.

What are the subscription plans?▾

Alpha ($99/mo) — 2 strategies, 1 broker. Pro ($189/mo) — 5 strategies, 3 brokers. Elite ($250/mo) — 10+ strategies, unlimited brokers, priority support. Paper demo is free forever.

Can I cancel anytime?▾

Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel your subscription whenever you want from your Account page. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Does it work with prop firms?▾

Absolutely. All our strategies are designed to stay within prop firm drawdown limits — $2K on $50K accounts, $3K on $100K, and $4,500 on $150K. Traders have passed evals with Apex, TopStep, and My Funded Futures using our strategies.

How fast are trades executed?▾

Signals are sent to your broker within milliseconds. Orders typically fill in under 1 second. This is faster than any manual trader could execute.
